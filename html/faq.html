What to Expect: Your First Visit FAQ

Q: How long is the initial appointment?

A: Your first visit lasts approximately 2 to 2.5 hours, allowing time for a full diagnostic workup, body-systems review, traditional analysis, and personalized treatment planning.

Q: What should I bring?

A: Please bring any recent lab work, imaging reports, a list of current supplements and medications, and any relevant medical records. Intake forms will be sent to you in advance.

Q: Will I receive treatment during the first visit?

A: Yes, in most cases. Once the diagnostic evaluation is complete, you may receive a gentle introduction to one or more modalities—such as acupuncture, cupping, herbal therapy, or lifestyle recommendations—depending on your presentation and readiness.

Q: What should I wear?

A: Comfortable clothing is recommended. Gowns or draping will be provided as needed, and you’ll always be treated with the utmost care and professionalism.

Q: How long are follow-up visits?

A: Follow-up appointments typically last 60 minutes, with 30-minute sessions available for focused treatments such as maintenance acupuncture, cupping, or herbal consults.

Q: Is this covered by insurance?

A: Depending on your provider and plan, some treatments may be eligible for reimbursement. Superbills can be provided upon request, and flexible payment plans are available for longer-term care programs.
